About

Two markets. One operator. A long-term thesis.

I operate at the intersection of Europe and Africa — two systems that should exchange more value, move faster, and understand each other with greater precision.

The Story

I was born in Burundi, where informal trade, scarcity, and resilience shape daily economic reality. Moving to Belgium exposed me to structured systems, regulated markets, and institutional capital.

This contrast is not just background — it is my operating advantage. I understand both the improvisational logic of African SMEs and the structured logic of European systems.

My focus is simple: reduce friction between these two worlds. Less theory. More execution.
